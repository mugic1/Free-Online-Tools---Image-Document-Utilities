Online PDF Splitter

Upload your PDF file and choose how you want to split it: extract all pages, select specific pages, or split into ranges.

How to Split a PDF:

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Choose your desired split option (e.g., extract specific pages).
  3. If applicable, enter the page numbers or ranges.
  4. Click "Split PDF".
  5. Download your new PDF files (often as a ZIP archive).

Features:

  • Split PDF into single pages.
  • Extract specific pages by number.
  • Split by custom page ranges.
  • Preview pages before splitting (advanced feature).
