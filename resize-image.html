Online Image Resizer

Upload your image and specify the new dimensions in pixels or as a percentage to resize it.

How to Resize Your Image:

  1. Upload your image file.
  2. Enter the desired width and/or height, or a scale percentage.
  3. Choose whether to maintain the aspect ratio.
  4. Click "Resize Image".
  5. Download your resized image.

Features:

  • Resize by pixels or percentage.
  • Option to maintain aspect ratio.
  • Supports various image formats.
  • Simple and quick.
