Online Image Compressor

Upload your image (JPG, PNG, GIF) to reduce its file size. Select your desired compression level for the best balance between size and quality.

How to Compress Your Image:

  1. Click "Choose image to compress" or drag and drop your file.
  2. Adjust the compression level if needed.
  3. Click the "Compress Image" button.
  4. Download your optimized image.

Features:

  • Supports JPG, PNG, and GIF formats.
  • Adjustable compression levels.
  • Fast and easy to use.
  • Securely processes your files.
